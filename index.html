Welcome to [POETIC-EXILE]. The domain is not a reflection of this worldbuild. It is more of a permanent placeholder for [NOURANTHYS]. The responsiveness should function smoothly. However, if any issues occur, please do contact me with a detailed description of the problem.

[POETIC-EXILE] serves as my digital archive of a worldbuild I never completely finished. It has been an ongoing project of mine for the past four years. I do not see myself ever completing this project, for the reason being that worldbuilding never has a true end.

NAVIGATING POETIC-EXILE

All of the navigation you require to roam this archive is included in the navigation bar above. Each link leads to a new category. The sidebars also include some links. Each category includes more sub-categories, and the list goes on. Entry pages may also include pop-up modals, external links, and hidden links within the website that are not listed in the navigation.

To get the most out of your stay here, I suggest you briefly scan the entries in the Lore Notes subcategory for context. The [READ ME] file may interest you with some insight into the information you will be exposed to here.

FUNCTIONALITY

This website is hosted by Github Pages. Due to the limited options as a static website, you may expect a majority of the pages to load slowly and possibly crash due to the abundance of code and heavy reliace on JS & CSS performance. I urge that you do not spam-click links or click through pages too quickly to ensure the page does not crash.
